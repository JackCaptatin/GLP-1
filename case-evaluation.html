🛡️ About the Lawsuits

GLP-1 drugs were not originally designed for weight loss — they were developed to treat diabetes. But when weight loss became a visible side effect, these drugs were heavily marketed to a broader population. Many users were never warned of the risk of life-altering complications.

Our Legal Action Aims to:

  • Hold drugmakers accountable
  • Compensate victims for medical bills, lost wages, and pain and suffering
  • Prevent future harm by pushing for stronger safety disclosures
