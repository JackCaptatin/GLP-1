Types of Cases We Handle

GLP-1 Medication Injuries

We represent clients who have suffered serious side effects from GLP-1 medications, including Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, and similar drugs. Our team has experience with a wide range of complications:

Gastroparesis Cases

Stomach paralysis requiring surgical intervention, feeding tubes, or permanent lifestyle changes. Many of our clients have required gastric pacemakers or other surgical procedures.

Bowel Obstruction Claims

Emergency surgeries and hospitalizations due to intestinal blockages. These cases often involve significant medical expenses and long recovery periods.

Intestinal Tissue Death

Cases involving necrosis requiring surgical removal of dead tissue and permanent ileostomy bags. These are among the most serious complications.

Pancreatitis Lawsuits

Life-threatening inflammation of the pancreas requiring hospitalization and intensive medical treatment. Some cases result in permanent organ damage.

Vision Loss Claims

Cases involving optic nerve damage (NAION) and other vision complications. These injuries can be permanent and significantly impact quality of life.

Mental Health Issues

Cases involving depression, suicidal thoughts, and other mental health complications linked to GLP-1 medication use.

Current Litigation Status

As of June 2025, there are 1,882 active lawsuits consolidated in the GLP-1 MDL (Multidistrict Litigation) in the Eastern District of Pennsylvania. Our firm is actively representing clients in this litigation and has extensive experience with the specific legal and medical issues involved.
