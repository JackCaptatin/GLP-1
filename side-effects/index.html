Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most common side effects?

The most commonly reported serious side effects include gastroparesis, pancreatitis, and gallbladder disease.

When should I seek medical attention?

Seek immediate medical attention if you experience severe abdominal pain, persistent vomiting, or vision changes.
